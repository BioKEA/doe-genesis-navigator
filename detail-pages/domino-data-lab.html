Introduction

Domino is a dual use AI/MLOps software and services provider. We specialize in fostering the collaborative development and deployment of ML / AI, Simulation, and Statistical Research models in complex environments including air gapped on premise, GovCloud, hybrid and multi-cloud. Further we integrate flexible governance throughout the software to help our customers balance innovation and risk mitigation.

Domino supports over 20% of the F100 including most of the top 20 life sciences companies, federal civ agencies, the intelligence community, FSIs, and the Department of Defense including classified work.

Domino maintains a team of forward deployed engineers who can work in concert with our customer subject matter experts and / or consulting firms / system integrators where we embed our experts into larger teams in order to deliver outcomes.

Domino is prepared to provide solutions to the following Genesis Challenges:

>Accelerating Materials Discovery for Strategic Deterrence (#12)

>Scaling the Biotechnology Revolution (#3)

>Designing Materials with Predictable Functionality (#14)

Finally, Domino is an open architecture, MOSA system that can either host capabilities inside a container or connect to virtually any tool or system over API. We are focused on partnering with integrators and tech alliances to deliver outcomes in phase II submissions.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

Domino can host any ML/AI tools and models across commercial and open source as well as software packages and libraries. Models can be built on our software either with Domino support or independetly.

AI/ML/Data science expertise

In addition to having built a leading AI/MLOps software platform with inputs for the most sophisticated public and private sectors organizations for over a decade, Domino employes world class AI/ML professional services personnel.

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

Domino is a kubernetes native software that can orchestrate compute anywhere that is K8s compatible including on prem, cloud, hybrid and multi-cloud. 

Datasets or data infrastructure

Domino has native integrations with every common data store and can connect to data anywhere. We do not own or centralize your data in Domino however.

Relevant Projects

Domino is on a production contract with the Navy. As part of this contract we are a prime managing 4 subcontractor - MLOps point solutions. Further we support university affiliated research centers, defense contractors and government operators share a data source but wall off their model IP in support or the program since we are highly flexible in how we control collaboration v. IP protection. The scope of this project is AI / Autonomy - maintaining ML pipelines for far edge devices including Unmanned Underwater Vehicles and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles.

Domino support a large intelligence agency accelerate ML/AI deployment across a very large user base while applying governance controls. Further, we are deploying across several on-premise, air gapped super computers with the ability to burst up to GovCloud.

Domino supports roughly 1,000 users at AstraZeneca and helps them accelerate drug discovery and clinical trial execution.
