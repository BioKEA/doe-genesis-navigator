Project Idea Summary

Manifested AI as Co-Scientist 

Allowing for rapid ideation and iteration using AI as co-scientist in a form other than text/screen. This addresses the challenge that innovation can suffer from the need for innovators to have to code or perform manual tasks that can now be delegated to AI agents, thus unleashing inventiveness and cognitive flourishing in the human user.  Need for AI engineering experts, researchers as domain experts, testbed/ sandbox of a pool of users, assistance with capacity building and implementation. 

 

Frontier Registry & Research Sphere (Prototypes and MVP already existing)

Embedded research integrity tool that authenticates data provenance, whether synthetic or human origin, and registers it onchain (on the blockchain) immutably. Intellectual Property origin is also traceable through the same blockchain mechanism whether or not using test-time compute. 

ResearchSphere facilitates interregional and interdisciplinary knowledge sharing among domain experts. It provides transparent, safe and protected means to share intellectual property and expertise with traceable guardrails among researchers. A 'LinkedIN for STEM researchers.'
