Introduction

My research focuses on epitaxy and device fabrication of wide bandgap semiconductors (GaN, Ga2O3, GeO2) for photonics and electronics. We have developed high voltage GaN HEMT and GaN vertical PN diodes. We are capable of growing Ga2O3 and GeO2 by MOCVD while the GeO2 is an emerging UWBG semiconductor.

The University of Utah is a top-tier public research university and AAU member with a rapidly growing, mission-driven research enterprise aligned with the goals of the Genesis Mission. The university is a national leader in subsurface energy through FORGE, operates a research reactor supporting advanced nuclear systems, and leads the NSF-funded WIRED Global Center focused on resilient grid infrastructure. Utah’s POWDER testbed enables cutting-edge wireless and spectrum research, while CORE-CM advances domestic critical minerals supply chains. Additional strengths include advanced manufacturing, a leading academic medical center, and the Peak Water Research Hub addressing water scarcity and energy-water systems.
