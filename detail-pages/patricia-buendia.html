Introduction

Lifetime Omics is a bioinformatics and health‑informatics company that models living organisms as multidimensional datasets, decoded through AI‑driven life sciences. In healthcare, we view human health through the lens of the “Pan-Ome”, the totality of genomic, environmental, and clinical data. We are currently scaling FAIRLYZ, a pioneering technology designed to provide researchers with access to a network of high-fidelity omics data.
