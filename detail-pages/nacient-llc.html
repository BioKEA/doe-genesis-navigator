    Introduction

    Nacient LLC is a specialized technology firm focused on enabling trusted, production-grade AI systems through secure architecture, identity governance, and real-world AI implementation. 

    Our leadership combines:

    - Enterprise-scale identity and cloud architecture expertise
    - Proven delivery of AI systems in regulated environments

    Most recently, one of our team members led the architecture and delivery of an AI-powered PBM Contract Intelligence platform for a Fortune 5 healthcare organization achieving 95%+ extraction accuracy and driving $20M+ annualized value through automated contract analysis and insight generation.

    We bring a practical, deployment-first perspective to AI, ensuring systems are not only innovative, but accurate, governed, and operationally viable. 

    We engage as a specialized technical partner, contributing to AI platform architecture, governance, and deployment workstreams within larger multi-institution teams.

    Project Idea Summary

    We enable trusted AI systems that convert complex, unstructured data into accurate, governed, decision-ready intelligence, with human-in-the-loop validation and secure, identity-integrated access.

    Aligned to AI for Scientific Reasoning, AI-Driven Autonomous Laboratories, and Cybersecurity for AI-driven Science Workflows, we propose a governed AI-driven knowledge extraction and reasoning layer that transforms scientific and operational artifacts (e.g., reports, experimental logs, regulatory documents) into structured, queryable data.

    Building on proven delivery of a production AI platform achieving 95%+ extraction accuracy in a regulated environment, this approach combines:

    - High-accuracy AI extraction and schema-driven structuring
    - Human-in-the-loop validation for traceability and trust
    - Identity-integrated access controls for secure, multi-party collaboration

    This enables faster data-to-decision cycles, improved reproducibility, and trusted AI adoption.

    We are seeking partners who can contribute domain-specific datasets, scientific expertise, and access to research workflows or experimental environments to validate and scale this capability while ensuring systems are accurate, governed, and deployable in high-consequence environments.
