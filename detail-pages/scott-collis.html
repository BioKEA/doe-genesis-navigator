Introduction

Atmospheric Scientist, Facility Director and Leader. 

I am an observationalist with a deep background in radar and other active remote sensing. I have, in the past mainly studied thunderstorms but recently have explored new areas including urban meteorology. I lead the world class Python ARM Radar Toolkit (Py-ART) which is the leading open source software package for working with weather radar data and is used in a lot of AI applications. I have also been actively engaged in AI at the Edge projects and I am very interested in self driving laboratories for earth system science

Project Idea Summary

My interests lies at the intersection between the water and autonomous laboratories subjects. I've seen a lot of example of self driving labs in biological sciences but none in Atmospheric sciences. I am very interested in using AI to supercharge the way we observe the atmosphere. I am interested in software defined sensors. Eg steerable LiDAR and Radar. Especially phased array radar which will greatly benefit from AI driven smart sensing using a digital twin.
