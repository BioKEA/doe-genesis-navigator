Introduction

Veritone is a global leader in enterprise AI, delivering innovative, human-centered solutions designed to augment complex decision-making and optimize operations at scale. Our Public Sector business unit specializes in providing advanced AI-powered data collection, processing, analysis, and visualization for federal, defense, and intelligence agencies. Through our award-winning aiWARE platform, iDEMS suite, and Assess solutions, Veritone seamlessly integrates generative and agentic AI into mission workflows, empowering organizations to accelerate insight generation, enhance situational awareness, and support critical national security objectives. Backed by deep domain expertise and a commitment to ethical AI, Veritone partners with government agencies to address today’s most challenging information environments—driving operational efficiency, informed decision-making, and mission success across the intelligence community and Department of Defense. As part of the Genesis Mission Consortium, Veritone is dedicated to fostering secure, interoperable, and scalable AI ecosystems in support of joint, all-domain missions.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

Veritone delivers cutting-edge AI models, tools, and software, including aiWARE, an enterprise AI platform for multimodal data analysis; the iDEMS suite for advanced data extraction, enrichment, and monitoring; and Assess for streamlined triage and review. Our solutions feature robust NLP, computer vision, generative AI, and agentic AI, enabling secure, rapid insights from structured and unstructured data for mission-critical government applications.

AI/ML/Data science expertise

Veritone brings deep AI/ML and data science expertise, including natural language processing, computer vision, audio analysis, generative and agentic AI, and federated learning. Our specialists design, train, fine-tune, and deploy custom and COTS models for mission data fusion, rapid decision support, and large-scale automation, ensuring secure, responsible, and effective AI solutions for the public sector and intelligence community.

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

Veritone offers a scalable, secure cloud and hybrid computing infrastructure tailored for public sector needs. Our platforms support on-premises, cloud-native, and edge deployments, leveraging leading commercial and government clouds. We ensure compliance with FedRAMP and DoD security standards, providing robust data protection, high availability, and seamless integration for distributed, mission-critical AI workloads.

Datasets or data infrastructure

Veritone’s data infrastructure supports ingestion, normalization, and secure storage of multimodal datasets, including audio, video, text, and sensor data. Our solutions enable rapid indexing, search, and fusion of structured and unstructured information, while maintaining rigorous data governance and chain-of-custody. We deliver scalable, API-driven access and robust tools for secure, mission-ready data management and analytics.

Other resources/tools

Veritone’s aiWARE platform delivers custom AI solutions rapidly and at scale through modular, interoperable architecture. It enables seamless integration of third-party and proprietary AI models, orchestrates automated workflows, and supports cloud, on-prem, and edge environments. This flexibility accelerates deployment, reduces total cost of ownership, and ensures mission agility while meeting advanced capability and interoperability demands.

Partnership Request & Project Idea

Partner Type Seeking
Non-profit
Academic institution
DOE National Lab
Capabilities Seeking
Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

We are seeking partners with a strong understanding of Department of Energy computing infrastructure needs, including experience with high-performance computing (HPC), cloud and hybrid environments, and secure data architectures. Ideal partners will be familiar with DOE security, compliance, and scalability requirements, as well as the deployment and management of advanced workloads in government, research, and scientific settings.

Datasets or data infrastructure

We are seeking partners with expertise in managing, curating, and maintaining large-scale scientific or energy-related datasets, including structured and unstructured data. Ideal collaborators will demonstrate proficiency in secure, scalable data infrastructure design, advanced data integration, normalization, and governance, as well as experience enabling API-driven access and robust analytics for research and mission-critical applications.

Subject matter expertise

Veritone seeks partners with deep subject-matter expertise in scientific domains relevant to the Department of Energy (DoE), particularly in energy systems, advanced research, or applied technologies. Ideal collaborators will have strong backgrounds in data management, processing, and analytics, including the ability to curate, interpret, and leverage complex scientific datasets. Additionally, experience in applying AI/ML to scientific and energy challenges, designing interoperable solutions, and operating in secure government environments is preferred.

Project Idea Summary

We are specifically seeking partners who:

Possess deep DOE domain expertise, particularly in export-control regulation, legacy records management, and nuclear industry compliance.

Have experience working with large, unstructured document repositories and sensitive data marking/release processes.

Can contribute subject matter expertise on DOE-specific workflows, datasets, or legacy systems to ensure successful AI model training and validation. 

Focus Area 4.H: Review, Classification, and Release of Legacy Documents
We seek partners with deep domain knowledge in DOE regulatory frameworks and export control, as well as expertise in legacy document management and sensitive information review. Ideal collaborators are familiar with complex text analytics, data classification, and release protocols for nuclear industry records. 

Target Focus Area 6.A: EM AI R&D Roadmap Implementation
We are looking for partners skilled in handling large-scale, historical scientific and operational data, particularly in the context of environmental management. Desired partners will offer proven experience in legacy data curation, multi-modal data conversion, and implementing standardized, interoperable data infrastructure.
