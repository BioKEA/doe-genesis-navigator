Introduction

I seek collaborations with domain scientists, applied mathematicians, and industry partners to build end-to-end, AI-integrated scientific workflows for power-grid analysis, scientific reasoning, complex flow systems, and atmospheric and hydrological prediction in energy technologies. I provide the mathematical and computational core: uncertainty quantification and inverse problems for stochastic PDE/DAE models, agentic workflows, probabilistic surrogates, and hybrid AI-physics methods grounded in scientific numerics. This includes grid uncertainty and operations, statistical treatment of stochastic inverse problems, differentiable PDE and neural operator approaches for compressible flow modeling, and a provenance-preserving verification framework that translates natural-language research claims into auditable formal artifacts, checks them using a trusted local reasoning core with symbolic mathematics and comparative proof tools, and returns explicit assumptions, missing premises, and open proof obligations.

I seek collaborations with domain scientists, applied mathematicians, and industry partners to build AI-integrated scientific workflows for power-grid analysis, scientific reasoning, complex flows, and atmospheric/hydrological prediction in energy technologies. I provide the mathematical core: UQ and inverse problems for stochastic PDE/DAE models, agentic workflows, probabilistic surrogates, and hybrid AI-physics methods.
