    Introduction

    The UNC Chapel Hill Sustainable Energy Research Consortium (UNC-SERC) is powering energy workforce development and technological innovations through research and training in sustainable energy science.

    Offerings & Experience

    Offerings
    AI models, tools, or software

    The Experiment Orchestration System (EOS): an open-source software framework and runtime for laboratory automation, designed to serve as the foundation for automated or self-driving labs (SDLs). https://unc-robotics.github.io/eos/

    AI/ML/Data science expertise

    Pptimization of experiment parameters. Signal processing algorithm and machine learning model development for time-series (sensor) signals

    Subject matter expertise

    (Photo)electrochemistry, catalysis, materials fabrication, surface chemistry

    Other expertise

    Robotics, automation, AI. Analog and digital electronics development, mechatronic system integration (motors, pumps, valves, etc.), embedded software development, mechanical design (Solidworks)
