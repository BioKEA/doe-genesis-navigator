Introduction

Dell Technologies is a trusted strategic partner to government and energy organizations, delivering secure, mission-ready infrastructure, modern data center and multicloud solutions, and AI-enabled services that help agencies accelerate innovation while protecting critical assets. With one of the industry’s broadest portfolios across compute, storage, networking and edge, and a deep commitment to cybersecurity, compliance, and sustainability, Dell works side by side with Department of Energy missions like Genesis to modernize operations, unlock the value of data, and ensure resilient, efficient IT that’s ready for what’s next.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

Dell offers end-to-end AI capabilities spanning secure on-prem, edge, and multicloud environments. We provide AI-optimized infrastructure (GPUs, storage, networking), a validated Dell AI Factory stack with MLOps tooling, optimized platforms for GenAI and RAG, and APEX Cloud Platforms for consistent operations. Our AI services and consulting help design, build, and operationalize models, ensuring secure, compliant AI tailored to mission and data requirements.

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

Dell delivers complete computing and cloud infrastructure, from secure on-prem data centers to edge and multicloud. We provide PowerEdge servers optimized for AI/HPC, PowerStore/PowerScale/ObjectScale storage, and high-performance networking to move and protect large data sets. With Dell APEX Cloud Platforms and as-a-Service, organizations can run workloads on-prem or in colocation with public cloud integration, enabling consistent operations, cost control, and scalability for mission-critical environments.

Datasets or data infrastructure

Dell supports end-to-end data infrastructure for mission data sets, from high-throughput ingest at the edge to secure long-term retention. We offer PowerStore and PowerScale for high-performance structured/unstructured data, ObjectScale and ECS for cloud-scale object storage, and APEX services for as-a-service consumption. Together with data protection, cyber recovery, and AIOps observability, Dell enables resilient, compliant data lakes and analytics platforms that keep critical DOE data available and ready for AI.

Subject matter expertise

Dell brings deep subject matter expertise across AI, HPC, cybersecurity, multicloud, and data infrastructure, with specialists who have decades of experience supporting U.S. federal and energy missions. Our consulting, services, and engineering teams help design architectures, optimize complex workloads, and operationalize secure AI and data platforms, aligning solutions with DOE mission, regulatory, and sustainability requirements while accelerating time to value.

Capabilities Seeking
AI/ML/Data science expertise

For Mission Genesis, Dell would look for AI/ML and data science experts who can advance DOE’s scientific AI and HPC goals on GPU-accelerated supercomputers and the American Science Cloud; build autonomous lab and robotics capabilities for closed-loop experimentation; develop foundational and domain AI models (including RAG over DOE data) that are trustworthy and explainable for national security, energy and grid planning; and design data/ML engineering pipelines that handle petabyte-scale data with secure, compliant governance across all 17 national labs and partners.

Project Idea Summary

Our project idea is to help DOE accelerate AI-driven science and national security by co-designing a mission-ready AI/HPC reference architecture that can be replicated across the Genesis ecosystem. We would focus on the “AI models and data infrastructure for the American Science Cloud” challenge area: building secure, scalable patterns for training and deploying domain AI models (including RAG and agents) on shared DOE data and multi-lab infrastructure.

In a partner, we are specifically seeking:

A DOE lab or research group with a concrete Genesis-aligned use case (e.g., grid planning, fusion, materials, accelerators, autonomous labs) and access to representative scientific data.

A partner willing to co-develop and validate end-to-end workflows—from data ingest and curation through model training, evaluation, and deployment on DOE-class systems.

Collaborators with strong scientific domain expertise who can define success metrics, stress-test model reliability and explainability, and help ensure the patterns we build are broadly reusable across multiple Genesis challenges and facilities.
