Introduction

 

Blokire Corporation (Blokire.io) - Build Intelligent Data Pipelines at Scale is an infrastructure-focused technology firm specializing in Isotropic Compute Substrates and Non-Orthogonal Signal Routing for advanced AI and Quantum-ready hardware. Our core R&D focuses on solving the "Energy Tax" inherent in traditional 2nm ASIC designs by implementing crystalline symmetry architectures that reduce timing jitter and parasitic capacitance by a validated 30%.

Capabilities for the Genesis Mission:

Hardware Architecture: Proprietary ASIC signal pathing and Thermal Fractal Conduit systems designed for high-density, energy-native AI environments.

Simulation & Design: Onboarded with the Synopsys Cloud Startup Program for 1/2nm DRC and GDSII sign-off.

Data Infrastructure: Through our active MVP platforms, Blokire.io - Build Intelligent Data Pipelines at Scale and SocialLabel.app, we provide vertically integrated "Human-in-the-Loop" data curation, specializing in high-fidelity ground truth for Physical AI, HPC, and industrial digital twins.

We are seeking to collaborate with National Labs and Academic Institutions under Phase I & II framework to validate our substrate's thermal and logic performance using high-performance computing (HPC) resources. Blokire is committed to establishing secure, domestically produced, and energy-efficient compute fabric for the Sovereign AI era.
