Introduction

Datyra, Inc. is a data science and artificial intelligence consulting firm based in Austin, TX and Oaxaca, MX. We have an expansive portfolio of leading projects that can be showcased to validate our firm's track record. We are seeking academic partners and DOE research labs to work with on the Genesis Mission Bid.
