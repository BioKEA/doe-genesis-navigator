    Introduction

    I am a Professor of Chemical Engineering at Illinois Institute of Technology. We work on molecular modeling of separation processes to identify promising membranes for a range of separations using reverse osmosis, ion-exchange, adsorption, etc.  We then use AI tools to optimize the processes sequence and suitability for a desired separation. Once that is done we work with our industrial partners to get the technology assessed and validated
