Introduction

Seekr Technologies is a leading AI technology company at the forefront of large language model (LLM) innovation, with a platform specifically designed to build, run, and monitor AI models and agents. Seekr’s models are built from the ground up with transparency and contestability in mind. Through its flagship platform, SeekrFlow, the company enables organizations to fine-tune and deploy LLMs across secure environments, using proprietary data pipelines and patented tools to generate high-quality, domain-specific training data. With active deployments across commercial and government sectors, collaborations with major hardware providers like AMD, NVIDIA and Intel, and the ability to operate in air-gapped environments on stand-alone hardware & appliances, Seekr stands as a premier provider of generative and agentic AI solutions that meet the highest standards for trust, accuracy, and mission alignment. 

Unlike many general-purpose AI companies, Seekr was founded under the principles of developing and deploying AI that is trustworthy, reliable, and transparent. With decades of experience in building search and intelligence systems for the defense and intelligence community, Seekr has developed proprietary tools — including its patented Seekr Score — to detect bias, misinformation, and subtle cognitive distortions across over 10 billion digital artifacts including text, image and audio content.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

Seekr provides trusted foundational AI models, vision language model capabilities, and agentic AI expert-level applications that consist of finely-tuned LLMs orchestrated to accomplish a user-generated objective. Seekr’s flagship platform, SeekrFlow, is our platform for configuring models, accessing user data, retraining open-weight LLMs via fine-tuning, and assessing their performance. SeekrFlow is an enterprise AI factory that builds, governs, and deploys domain specific AI agents rather than using traditional foundational LLMs or generic conversational models.

AI/ML/Data science expertise

Seekr currently holds 30 patents (14 granted and 16 pending), across key areas such as data science innovations, principle alignment, risk scoring documents, search systems and content delivery. Seekr has over 120 staff members, of which approximately 10% have achieved PhDs in fields related to AI, Data Science, Computer Science, and Natural Language Processing.

Relevant Projects

US Army SBIR A244-P037 AI/ML Open Topic: Through collaboration with Project Linchpin, Seekr is experimenting with LLM development to address IPB bottlenecks within TITAN, EWPMT, MFEW Air Large, ALE/GLE Small & Large systems. This is within the PEO Intelligence, Electronic Warfare & Sensors (IEW&S) portfolio. 

Global leader in semiconductor manufacturing. Customer is licensing Seekr’s AI development toolset for their developer cloud, enabling rapid onboarding of AI models onto their AI accelerator chip. The Seekr software also supports training and inferencing of LLMs and AI Expert systems on the customer's cloud infrastructure. This multi-year agreement offers AI model training and deployment at a fraction of the cost compared to other GPUs.

Leading supply chain analytics and monitoring company. A leader in defense technology, focusing on IoT sensor tracking to improve global supply chains engaged Seekr to add AI capabilities to analyze supply chain and IoT data. This aims to identify adversary intentions and reduce national security risks. Seekr provides AI Appliances that work independently from external data sources, ensuring the security and confidentiality of sensitive information.
