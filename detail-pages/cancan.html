Introduction

CANCAN is a Bakersfield-based organization specializing in high-purity biochemical extraction and automated environmental auditing. We have developed a proprietary bio-acoustic induction protocol for the precision extraction of pharmaceutical-grade peptides from biological biomass.

​Technical Capabilities:

​99.14% Purity Target: Validated via 10 Billion Stochastic Molecular Dynamics simulations identifying global optima for molecular expression.

​Non-Chemical Induction: Proprietary acoustic modulation sequences that drive high-yield protein expression with minimal environmental overhead.

​Digital Twin Infrastructure: Automated auditing via Hybrid YOLOv12 + Vision Transformer (ViT) vision systems for real-time biomass characterization and 50-year fiscal/environmental forecasting.

​Partnership Objectives:

We are seeking Genesis Mission collaborators—specifically National Laboratories and Academic Research Centers—to provide high-fidelity biochemical characterization (LC-MS, NMR) to validate simulation results. We offer a "de-risked" IP asset ready for pilot-scale integration into sustainable resource recovery and bio-manufacturing hubs. CANCAN is a registered Food Recovery Organization (EIN 39-2261270, UEI SSWWJ3SEMZ73) operating under local compliance frameworks (BMC § 8.32.190).

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

We utilize a proprietary Hybrid YOLOv12 and Vision Transformer (ViT) architecture for real-time biological biomass characterization. This computer vision stack automates the categorization of feedstock inputs, ensuring high-fidelity metadata generation for downstream biochemical extraction processes and long-term environmental auditing.

AI/ML/Data science expertise

Expertise in stochastic molecular dynamics and large-scale simulation modeling. We have executed over 10 billion stochastic runs to identify global optima for peptide expression. Our team specializes in bridging computational predictions with physical bio-acoustic induction to maximize chemical yield and purity.

Datasets or data infrastructure

CANCAN maintains a "Digital Twin" infrastructure that logs high-resolution temporal and spectral data for every extraction cycle. This dataset includes biomass source tracking, acoustic resonance parameters, and 50-year fiscal/environmental forecasting models compliant with local regulatory frameworks (BMC § 8.32.190).

Subject matter expertise

Deep expertise in bio-acoustic induction and non-chemical biochemical extraction. We specialize in using specific acoustic modulation frequencies to trigger high-purity (99.14%) peptide expression. Additional expertise includes circular economy utility management and regional food recovery organization (FRO) compliance.

Relevant Projects

Project 1: Stochastic Bio-Acoustic Induction Optimization

​Scope: Developed a proprietary 650.13\text{ Hz} acoustic induction protocol for high-purity peptide extraction from biological biomass.

​Achievement: Executed 10 Billion Stochastic Molecular Dynamics simulations to identify global optima for molecular expression, resulting in a validated 99.14\% target purity benchmark.

​Project 2: Autonomous Environmental Auditing (Digital Twin)

​Scope: Engineered a real-time monitoring and categorization system using Hybrid YOLOv12 and Vision Transformer (ViT) architectures.

​Achievement: Successfully deployed an automated auditing infrastructure (compliant with BMC § 8.32.190) that generates high-fidelity metadata for 50-year fiscal and environmental impact forecasting.

​Project 3: Regional Compliance & Resource Recovery (CANCAN)

​Scope: Established a registered Food Recovery Organization (FRO) focused on bio-integrated utility management in Kern County, CA.

​Achievement: Secured operational authority for large-scale resource recovery at the 1501 Pearl St and Panama Lane nodes, integrating local regulatory frameworks with advanced biochemical production.

Project Idea Summary

Project Title: Bio-Acoustic Peptide Synthesis: AI-Validated Purity for Circular Economy Initiatives

​Technical Focus: We are scaling a proprietary, non-chemical induction protocol for the high-purity extraction of biochemicals (peptides) from biological biomass. This project bridges the gap between AI-accelerated discovery and industrial-scale biomanufacturing.

​The Assets: Our process is optimized through 10 Billion Stochastic Molecular Dynamics simulations, identifying global optima for 99.14% target purity. We utilize a Hybrid YOLOv12 + ViT computer vision stack for autonomous auditing and categorization, creating a "Digital Twin" metadata trail compliant with local environmental frameworks.

​What We Seek: We are seeking a DOE National Laboratory or Academic partner to provide high-fidelity experimental validation. Our primary needs are access to BSL-rated wet lab facilities (sterile hoods/bioreactors) and analytical instrumentation (LC-MS/NMR) to structurally verify our bio-acoustic extraction batches.

​Mission Alignment: This project directly supports the Genesis Mission goal of "Scaling the Biotechnology Revolution" by providing a de-risked, AI-driven pathway for sustainable resource recovery and advanced manufacturing. We are ready to co-author Phase I proposals (DE-FOA-0003612) and integrate our IP into larger multidisciplinary teams.
