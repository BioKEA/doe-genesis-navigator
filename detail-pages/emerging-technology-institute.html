Introduction

Emerging Technology Institute (ETI) is a mission-driven organization specializing in the design, development, and deployment of advanced software solutions that enable real-world application of emerging technologies. Our core capabilities include AI/ML systems, data integration platforms, and scalable software architectures that support rapid development, testing, and operational deployment.

Aligned with the goals of the Genesis Mission Consortium, ETI focuses on accelerating innovation by bridging software development with applied research and field implementation. Our platforms are built to support complex, data-driven environments across government, industry, and academic collaboration.

Beyond software, ETI integrates robotics, unmanned systems, and advanced technology testing with workforce training and STEM education. This approach allows us to not only build impactful solutions, but also develop the skilled workforce and ecosystems required to sustain them.

As a consortium partner, ETI brings the capability to translate emerging technologies into scalable, deployable systems supporting AI-driven discovery, collaborative research, and national innovation priorities.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI/ML/Data science expertise

ETI delivers advanced AI/ML and data science capabilities focused on autonomous systems, including model training, optimization, and real-time integration for robotics and drones. The team combines expertise in autonomy development, data-driven decision-making, and onboard computing optimization to enable responsive, mission-ready systems. Key personnel include Antonio Ball (AI and autonomy training/integration), Tyuss Handley (data science and autonomy integration), and Jason Robinson (UI development and real-time onboard systems optimization).

Experimental or test facilities

ETI is a multi-time host of USSOCOM T&E events, conducting multi-day conferences with 60+ vendors for large-scale, real-world technology validation. ETI provides secure experimental facilities including a high-security sandbox with unrestricted airspace, CNC machining, and 3D printing, along with flexible indoor/outdoor test areas. The X8 Lab enables hands-on prototyping, experimentation, and collaboration.

Subject matter expertise

James Freeman: Entrepreneurship, consulting, and defense contracting with a focus on T&E and solution development

Antonio Ball: AI and autonomy training, optimization, and integration for robotics and drones

Tyuss Handley: Data science, AI, autonomy, and integration into robotics and drone systems

Jason Robinson: UI development and optimization of onboard computing for real-time decision-making

AI models, tools, or software

ETI seeks partners that provide advanced AI models, specialized software tools, and data science capabilities to support development, training, and deployment of autonomous systems, including real-time decision-making and integration with robotics and drones.

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

ETI seeks access to scalable cloud and computing infrastructure to support model training, simulation, data processing, and deployment of AI-enabled systems.

Datasets or data infrastructure

ETI seeks partners with access to high-quality, domain-relevant datasets and robust data infrastructure to enable effective model training, validation, and continuous improvement.

Subject matter expertise

ETI seeks collaborators with domain expertise, integration support, and complementary technologies that enhance T&E, accelerate development, and improve mission-ready outcomes.

Project Idea Summary

ETI’s project focuses on advancing AI-enabled autonomous systems for real-time decision-making in robotics and drone operations, with an emphasis on test and evaluation (T&E) in realistic operational environments. The challenge area centers on improving model performance, integration, and validation under dynamic, mission-relevant conditions. ETI is seeking partners that provide advanced AI models, scalable computing infrastructure, and access to high-quality datasets, as well as complementary technologies and test assets to accelerate development and enable robust, real-world validation.
