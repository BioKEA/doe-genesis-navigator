Introduction

World Wide Technology (WWT), honored as NVIDIA’s 2026 Federal Partner of the Year, is a global leader in technology solutions, driving innovation across industries. With a strong focus on AI, WWT leverages its decade-long research and development in AI/ML to enhance customer experiences and deliver transformative solutions. The company offers AI-powered application development that revolutionize user experiences and employs practical AI strategies to accelerate GenAI adoption while mitigating risks.

WWT’s capabilities extend beyond AI, encompassing robust cybersecurity and networking solutions. The company collaborates with over 200 partners, including Cisco, Dell, and NVIDIA, to provide comprehensive AI security solutions and scalable AI infrastructure. WWT’s Cyber Range offers hands-on cybersecurity training, while its networking solutions decrease attack surfaces and improve operational efficiency.

The North American Integration Center (NAIC) at WWT is a 5 million square foot facility near Edwardsville, Illinois, designed for complex technology integration and deployment. It features a flexible design for high-volume system integration and a 24/7 virtual ecosystem for client collaboration. The NAIC offers comprehensive logistics, installation, and configuration services, it is part of a global network with centers in Amsterdam, Mumbai, and Singapore, enhancing WWT’s ability to meet diverse customer needs.

Relevant Projects

Revolutionizing Telecom Network Operations Through AI

WWT partnered with a leading wireless carrier to modernize its network operations using AI solutions. The collaboration aimed to transform the carrier’s Network Operations Center (NOC) from reactive to proactive, predictive intelligence. WWT’s expertise in AI, data analytics, automation, and telecommunications played a crucial role in this transformation, positioning the carrier as a leader in AI-powered network intelligence.

Key Phases:

Knowledge to Intelligence: Implemented a generative AI solution to interpret complex queries, empowering technicians with faster and more accurate issue resolution.

Real-time Insight: Integrated critical platforms into a unified ecosystem, enabling real-time data access and reducing tool-switching time for technicians.

Automation and Noise Reduction: Automated low-priority alarms, reducing manual workloads by up to 40%, allowing technicians to focus on higher-value tasks.

Outcomes:

Operational Excellence: Reduced manual workloads and elevated the role of technical experts.

Data-driven Decision Making: Improved metrics for mean time to resolution (MTTR).

Scalable Technology Foundation: Established a flexible AI architecture for enterprise-wide transformation.

https://www.wwt.com/case-study/powering-telecom-network-transformation-with-ai

Tactical Edge Computing Transforms Mission Capability for Defense Organization

WWT worked with a military organization to enhance its operational capabilities using a ruggedized, portable computing platform. The existing infrastructure was cumbersome, requiring significant setup time and power, and lacked connectivity, which limited operational efficiency.

Challenge:

The need for a secure, scalable computing solution at the tactical edge.

Solution:

WWT and Dell Federal developed a prototype using Dell’s XR4000 ruggedized servers.

The solution included a virtualized design with Commercial Solutions for Classified (CSfC) capabilities.

The prototype was tested in WWT’s Advanced Technology Center.

Results:

Deployment times reduced from hours to minutes.

Secure operational flexibility with tri-modal connectivity.

Cost savings through system consolidation.

Expanded deployment options due to reduced weight and footprint.

Real-time intelligence processing at the edge with GPU capabilities.

https://www.wwt.com/case-study/tactical-edge-computing-transforms-mission-capability-for-defense-organization

Scott Data Embraces AI Transformation to Offer GPU as a Service
