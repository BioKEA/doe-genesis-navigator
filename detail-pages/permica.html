    Introduction

    Permica is an AI company building intelligence infrastructure for federal energy permitting. Our IPRI platform (Infrastructure Permitting Risk Intelligence) applies agentic AI, retrieval-augmented generation, and continuous risk scoring to federal NEPA documents — Environmental Impact Statements, Environmental Assessments, FONSI decisions, and litigation records — enabling energy project developers, environmental consultants, and infrastructure financiers to assess permitting risk before submission. We serve hydrocarbons, geothermal, transmission, and other energy infrastructure sectors where NEPA compliance is a primary deployment bottleneck.

    Relevant Projects

    Project 1:

    NEPA Permitting Risk Intelligence Platform (IPRI) — Permica 2024–Present Designed and built an end-to-end AI platform for federal NEPA permitting risk analysis. The system ingests Environmental Impact Statements and Environmental Assessments from regulations.gov and related federal repositories, applies a multi-tier agentic extraction pipeline to structure unstructured regulatory text, and generates continuous risk scores (0.0–1.0) predicting permitting outcomes based on precedent-stratified retrieval. The platform incorporates RAG-based knowledge retrieval across NEPA precedents, litigation records, and federal policy guidance. Developed for energy infrastructure project developers, environmental consultants, and infrastructure financiers operating in hydrocarbons, geothermal, and transmission sectors.

    Project 2:

    Enterprise AI Data Architecture — Major Integrated Energy Company Led data platform architecture and AI pipeline design for a large-scale energy operations environment. Delivered Medallion architecture on Databricks with Unity Catalog governance, MLOps pipelines on GCP, and Vertex AI model deployment for operational analytics. Scope included integrating SCADA/OPC-UA industrial data streams with enterprise data lakes, enabling predictive maintenance and operational intelligence use cases across refining operations.

    Project 3:

    Enterprise Data Platform Modernization —  Architected and delivered a cloud-native data platform supporting subsurface and production data workflows. Implemented GCP-based BigQuery data warehouse, automated data quality pipelines, and AI-assisted analytics for upstream operations. Established data governance frameworks and MLOps standards enabling scalable model deployment across exploration and production business units.

    Project Idea Summary

    Permica is seeking partners to form a Phase I Genesis Mission team focused on the Unleashing Subsurface Strategic Energy Assets challenge, with relevance to grid scaling and AI for scientific reasoning.

    Project: AI-Accelerated NEPA Permitting Intelligence for U.S. Energy Infrastructure

    Federal NEPA environmental review is one of the most significant time and cost bottlenecks in U.S. energy infrastructure deployment — particularly for hydrocarbons, geothermal, and transmission projects. Permica has developed an AI platform that ingests federal NEPA documents (EIS, EA, FONSI filings), applies retrieval-augmented generation across a curated regulatory knowledge base, and produces continuous risk scores to predict permitting outcomes before submission.

    For this Genesis Mission Phase I project, we propose to demonstrate measurable AI advantage by: (1) scaling our document ingestion pipeline across the full federal NEPA corpus using national lab HPC resources; (2) developing and validating outcome-stratified AI models with academic partners; and (3) quantifying reduction in permitting analysis cycle time versus baseline manual review workflows.

    What we bring: Production AI platform, proprietary NEPA document extraction pipeline, energy sector domain expertise, and established industry relationships with energy project developers and environmental consultants.
