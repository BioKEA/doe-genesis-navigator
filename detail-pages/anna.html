Introduction

Skillit is America’s leading agentic hiring platform for construction.

AI is driving one of the largest infrastructure buildouts in modern history, yet 94% of contractors cite labor as the #1 constraint. Despite over $30B spent annually on tools, services, and recruiting operations, the supply of skilled craft labor remains fragmented and difficult to access.
 

To solve this, Skillit has built the largest network of vetted craft workers and a system of AI agents that onboard workers, run hiring workflows, and generate labor intelligence across the industry. Our agents source, qualify, and connect workers automatically, enabling contractors to hire dramatically faster at a fraction of the cost. As more hiring runs through Skillit, the platform becomes the infrastructure layer for construction hiring — powering how the industry finds, hires, and understands labor across every trade, region and labor type. We want to use our platform to power projects from the Genesis mission. 

Our area of work is crosscutting and fits teams that are interested in creating better hiring workflows for hard to fill construction infrastructure trade roles. Construction is key to accelerating scientific discovery and to bridge the "valley of death" between planning and execution. 

We are seeking partners that are interested in developing this area of work further.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

1. Agentic AI and Workflow Orchestration via our Agentic Workforce Platform
Skillit is the engine that can autonomously manage the deployment of specialized labor for complex R&D projects, functioning as a "closed-loop" discovery partner that adapts as the project’s technical needs evolve. For example, we currently help construction companies source very difficult to find roles. We can help bridge the gap between "scientific discovery" and commercially viable products and actual build outs. 

Datasets or data infrastructure

We are the first agentic platform to make construction skilled trades searchable and finable in the United States. As such, we have a unique and growing data set with a competitive edge. We have collaboration and data networks as well as proprietory hiring data. 

Subject matter expertise

Our subject matter expertise is agentic infrastructure provider for scientific and industrial R&D workflows. Our agents not only match institutions with workers, they reason through project requirements to match with specific workforces. Our tech layer allows us to   provide visibility into the "nuclear-ready" specialized workforce.

Project Idea Summary

We want to help power the growth of our partners and the Genesis Mission, and we want to contribute to scaling energy build outs.  We are seeking partners that have the following expertise: 

Nuclear Energy Infrastructure

1. EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction): Partners who understand the complexities of building large-scale nuclear power plants and can integrate Skillit's labor-matching into "construction-ready support".

2. Nuclear Reactor Vendors: Partners with expertise in the design and licensing of reactors (especially Small Modular Reactors) to ensure AI-driven workflows meet "rigorous standards for traceability, verification and validation".

3. Regulatory Compliance: Partners with knowledge of nuclear licensing pipelines to help our AI automate and enhance the preparation and review of safety and regulatory documentation.
