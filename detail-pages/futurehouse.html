Introduction

FutureHouse is a nonprofit research lab building an AI Scientist to automate and accelerate science that improves human health.

FutureHouse is a nonprofit independent research organization based in San Francisco, founded in 2023 by Sam Rodriques and Andrew White. We build and validate AI systems for scientific discovery — primarily in biology right now — and run initiatives that put those systems in the hands of exceptional scientists.

Our flagship program today is the AI-for-Science Independent Postdoctoral Fellowship, which pairs early-career researchers with advanced AI tools and academic co-advisors at leading research institutions. We also develop training resources to help scientists in academia integrate AI into their own research workflows.

FutureHouse focuses on use-inspired basic research — fundamental science motivated by real-world problems. Edison Scientific, a for-profit company that spun out of FutureHouse in 2025, commercializes the AI tools. FutureHouse has access to Edison's technology and remains focused on its nonprofit mission: accelerating discovery for the public good.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI/ML/Data science expertise

In our first two years, FutureHouse built the foundational infrastructure for AI-accelerated science: We built the first language agents for science and the first robust benchmarks for measuring language agents on scientific tasks. We produced the first example of an AI system making a validated novel discovery in biology; and we built one of the first reasoning models for science. We partner with basic science researchers to provide engineering support for their specific projects. 

Subject matter expertise

FutureHouse brings distinctive subject matter expertise at the intersection of AI, biology, and scientific infrastructure. Our model pairs exceptional scientific talent embedded in leading academic labs with dedicated software engineering support, enabling researchers to translate advanced AI methods into real biological discovery. Led by experienced scientific and operational leaders, we combine deep research credibility with the technical and organizational capacity to execute ambitious interdisciplinary programs.

Project Idea Summary

FutureHouse’s mission as an independent nonprofit organization is to ensure that academia and other non-profit research institutes stay at the cutting-edge of AI. We support the research community by building AI fluency, expanding access to key enabling capabilities such as software engineering and compute, and partnering with investigators to develop agentic systems that can automate and accelerate complex biological research workflows. We are particularly interested in collaborations with teams tackling high-impact scientific challenges, generating valuable datasets, and seeking technical partners to translate AI opportunities into deployable research infrastructure.
