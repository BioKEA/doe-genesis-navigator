Introduction

Tryfacta, Inc. is an SBA 8(a) and Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB) providing secure IT, AI, cybersecurity, cloud, and workforce solutions. With more than 29 years of experience supporting Federal, SLED, and healthcare markets, Tryfacta delivers mission-focused solutions that integrate technology, data, and operations. Core capabilities include AI and automation, cybersecurity, cloud modernization, digital transformation, enterprise IT services, data analytics, managed services, and low-code/no-code platform delivery.

Tryfacta is developing an AI-enabled framework to accelerate scientific discovery and operational optimization across DOE-relevant domains.
•    AI-enabled, physics-informed digital twin frameworks that unify simulation, experimental, and operational data into continuous, closed-loop scientific workflows.
•    Hybrid AI + physics-based modeling
•    Closed-loop workflows (prediction → validation → refinement)
•    Real-time integration of heterogeneous data sources
•    Scalable architectures aligned with DOE data ecosystems
This approach reduces time-to-insight, improves model fidelity, and enables scalable, mission-aligned deployment across complex DOE research and operational environments by continuously integrating data and models across the full research lifecycle.

Tryfacta will serve as the integration and execution lead, enabling end-to-end orchestration of data, models, and workflows across fragmented scientific environments.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI/ML/Data science expertise

Tryfacta applies AI/ML and data science techniques to enable predictive modeling, data fusion, and workflow automation across complex scientific and operational environments. Our focus is on integrating AI capabilities with domain-specific models and data sources to support physics-informed modeling, simulation, and continuous refinement. We emphasize practical application of AI within real-world constraints, enabling scalable and operationally viable solutions for scientific discovery and system optimization.

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

Tryfacta designs and implements scalable, secure computing architectures that support AI-enabled workloads across cloud, hybrid, and on-premises environments. Our capabilities include infrastructure planning, workload orchestration, and deployment of AI/ML pipelines within environments aligned to federal security and governance requirements. We focus on enabling reliable, high-performance execution of modeling, simulation, and data-intensive workloads across distributed systems.

Datasets or data infrastructure

Tryfacta provides data architecture and engineering capabilities to support the integration, management, and accessibility of large-scale, heterogeneous datasets. We design data pipelines that unify simulation, experimental, and operational data, enabling seamless data flow across systems and supporting AI-driven analysis. Our approach emphasizes data interoperability, governance, and scalability to support continuous scientific workflows.

Subject matter expertise

Tryfacta brings extensive experience supporting federal mission environments, including energy, defense, and scientific programs. Our subject matter expertise includes system integration, data-driven decision support, and execution of complex programs within regulated and high-security environments. We understand the operational and governance constraints associated with deploying advanced technologies within federal research and infrastructure settings.

Other expertise

Tryfacta specializes in the integration and orchestration of AI-enabled scientific workflows across distributed and multi-domain environments. We enable end-to-end connectivity between data sources, models, and computational platforms, supporting closed-loop workflows that integrate prediction, validation, and refinement. Our role focuses on bridging gaps between research, infrastructure, and operations to enable scalable, mission-aligned deployment.

Relevant Projects

Federal Data Integration & Workflow Enablement (NLRB)

Tryfacta supported the National Labor Relations Board (NLRB) in modernizing and integrating enterprise data and case management systems to improve data accessibility and operational workflows within a secure federal environment. The effort included development of data pipelines, integration of disparate systems, and implementation of scalable data architecture aligned with federal governance and security requirements.

Tryfacta unified previously siloed datasets and systems into a cohesive environment, improving data availability and enabling more efficient, streamlined workflows across multiple operational units. This integration enhanced decision-making by providing more timely and consistent access to critical data while reducing manual processes and system fragmentation.

The solution established repeatable data integration patterns and scalable architecture capable of supporting advanced analytics and future AI-enabled capabilities. This experience directly aligns with the Genesis Mission’s need to integrate diverse data sources and workflows across complex environments to enable scalable, AI-driven discovery and operational outcomes.
