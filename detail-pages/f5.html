Introduction

As the Genesis Mission is fulfilled, F5 provides the essential infrastructure to secure, deliver, and optimize the world’s most powerful scientific platforms. F5 ensures that the transition from pilot AI models to exascale-powered production environments is fast, resilient, and inherently secure.

Key AI Use Cases for the Genesis Mission:

AI Security & Guardrails: F5 provides automated "guardrails" to protect the American Science and Security Platform. We secure Large Language Model (LLM) interactions, preventing data leakage and ensuring AI insights remain aligned with ethical and regulatory standards.

High-Performance Data Pipelines: The Genesis Mission relies on massive datasets for fusion, fission, and materials science. F5 optimizes GPU utilization and AI Data Delivery by eliminating network bottlenecks and ensuring low-latency data movement between DOE’s distributed computing facilities.

Defending the American Science Cloud: As Genesis connects national labs, industry, and academia, F5’s platform provides a unified defense against automated threats and API abuse, safeguarding the integrity of DOE’s unique scientific data.

Hybrid Orchestration: F5 enables the seamless integration of on-premises supercomputers with cloud-native environments, providing consistent security across the entire DOE ecosystem.

F5 empowers the Genesis Mission to accelerate American research by making AI-driven science trustworthy, scalable, and mission-ready.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

F5 provides a specialized, model-agnostic software stack designed to secure and optimize the AI lifecycle within the Genesis Program. Our capabilities center on AI Runtime Security, providing real-time guardrails for any LLM to prevent prompt injection and sensitive data exfiltration. We offer integrated tools for automated agentic Red Teaming and observability, ensuring model integrity and compliance across the American Science Cloud. By streamlining the deployment of AI-native software and APIs, F5 enables researchers to rapidly scale trusted models from experimental pilots to production.

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

F5 delivers the high-performance infrastructure required to power the Genesis Program’s exascale ambitions. Through deep integration with NVIDIA, F5 optimizes AI workloads by ensuring seamless, low-latency data delivery, including multi-tenancy, to GPU clusters. Our platform provides the networking fabric for the American Science Cloud, enabling researchers to bridge on-premises supercomputing with hybrid-cloud environments. By maximizing hardware efficiency and securing the underlying connectivity, F5 ensures that the DOE’s massive computing resources are resilient, scalable, and mission-read

Other resources/tools

F5 provides a Zero Trust architecture and comprehensive API security to protect the Genesis Program’s critical data and AI services. By enforcing identity-based access and continuous authorization, we ensure that only verified users and applications can interact with DOE resources. Our platform secures the vast landscape of APIs connecting researchers to exascale models, defending against unauthorized access and sophisticated threats. With F5, the American Science Cloud maintains a resilient, secure-by-design posture that safeguards the integrity of national scientific innovation.

AI models, tools, or software

We seek AI model vendors or AI powered applications—building either highly secure or unrestrained models—to demonstrate the benefits of decoupled, programmable security. We aim to show how F5’s external guardrails allow any model to be safely and rapidly scaled to exascale production within the American Science Cloud while maintaining strict mission-aligned safety standards.

Experimental or test facilities

We are seeking collaborations with experimental and test facilities to test and/or demonstrate the secure, low-latency movement of sensitive research data to the American Science Cloud. We aim to show how F5 can protect high-fidelity data streams and remote instrument control, ensuring that AI-driven experiments remain resilient and secure from edge to exascale.

Project Idea Summary

The AI Factory Secure Transit Hub 

We propose a "Secure Transit Hub" that integrates high-speed data delivery with real-time AI runtime security. This solution optimizes multi-tenant AI factories by isolating sensitive data streams and providing programmable security guardrails directly at the ingestion point, ensuring model-agnostic protection without compromising performance. To accelerate the transition to production, the hub includes an integrated testing framework that subjects models and applications to a state-of-the-art agentic attack database. By automating adversarial testing and security validation, we significantly streamline the Authority to Operate (ATO) process for mission-critical AI systems.  This Hub can be deployed in cloud based deployments or disconnected secure environments

Partner Request: We seek partners operating experimental facilities or AI testbeds to co-develop and demonstrate this Secure Transit Hub. We are looking for facilities with high-fidelity, high-volume data streams (e.g., sensor arrays or remote instrumentation) to validate that F5 can deliver multi-tenant isolation and mission-critical security while maintaining the throughput required for real-time AI analysis at exascale. We specifically invite partners interested in pioneering automated security compliance and threat-emulation workflows to reduce the administrative burden of AI deployment.
