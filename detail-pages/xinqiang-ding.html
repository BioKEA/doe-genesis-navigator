    Introduction

    I am an assistant professor in the chemistry department at Tufts University. My lab's research focused on combining and developing molecular dynamics (MD) simulations and AI techniques for solving challenging problem in computational chemistry and biophysics. Currently, we are working on developing fast and accurate methods to compute protein-ligand absolute binding free energy for drug design and to predict fold-switching proteins and their multiple conformations.

    Before I started my independent lab, I got my PhD in computational chemistry from UMich and did postdoc training at MIT. I have years of research experiences on developing advanced computational methods by combining MD with ML/AI methods. One specific focus has been developing new free energy methods for computational drug design.

    On this platform, I am looking for partnerships and collaborations where I can contribute my expertise in using MD and ML/AI for solving challenging problems in biotechnology.
