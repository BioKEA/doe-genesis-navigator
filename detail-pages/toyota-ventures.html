Introduction

Toyota Ventures ($800M+ AUM) is Toyota’s first standalone early-stage venture capital firm, investing broadly across deep and climate tech, beyond the immediate intersection with the automotive sector. Specifically, the Climate Fund was launched in the summer of 2021, at the same time Toyota announced its carbon-neutrality goals. We invest in topics such as energy, materials, carbon management, and industrial decarbonization.

Subject matter expertise

Project Idea Summary

As Toyota's early-stage corporate venture fund, we are on a mission to discover what's next for Toyota. The Climate Fund is a $150M fund investing across nearly all areas of climate-tech. Being that we are financial first and strategic second, we have the dexterity to invest beyond the traditional automotive scope. Open to connecting with startups, national labs, and other organizations.
