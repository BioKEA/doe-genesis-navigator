Introduction

I am a renewable energy ecologist that has focused on a variety of wildlife/renewable energy issues. My focus taxa have ranged from bats, birds, and herpetofauna under various renewable energies such as wind, solar, and woody biomass. Most recently, I have been assessing bird and bat interactions with renewable energy facilities.
