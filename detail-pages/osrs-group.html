Introduction

Advanced software and systems for critical infrastructure and the environment

OSRS Group works with utilities, public agencies, and infrastructure operators to design and build high-reliability software and cloud systems for geospatial, temporal, and analytic workloads. We are taking on new consulting and development engagements while we grow our portfolio of R&D and IP.

What we do

Consulting, prototypes, and production-grade builds. We help teams move from ideas and legacy tools to working software:

    Architecture and development of cloud-native, geospatial, spatio-temporal, and AI-enabled systems

    High-assurance implementation and integration on modern stacks (e.g., .NET, Python, cloud, data services, AI/ML)

    Focused R&D and prototype efforts that can grow into operational capabilities

We do collaborative R&D with our clients principally in:

    Software:

        Analytics and Algorithm Design

        AI Enablement of Complex Systems and Analytic Workflows

        Big Data and Distributed Computing at Scale

        Cybersecurity and Information Assurance

        Geospatial and Location Based Technology

        Temporal, BiTemporal, TimeSeries and Space-Time Processes

    Processes and Systems Engineering:

        Organizational Change Management and Planning

        Operational / Business Process Modeling

        Software Engineering and Needs Planning and Management

        System of Systems Engineering

        AI-Augmented Decision-Support and Operational Modeling

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI/ML/Data science expertise

We develop advanced analytic and AI/ML methods for various technology domains, especially in the geospatial, defense, intelligence, utility, infrastructure and environmental domains.

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

We provide cloud hosting, colocation and on-prem / hybrid cloud services for customers working with our technologies as part of our other offerings. Our focus is cloud-native technologies and enabling an on-premises deployment without massive code changes. We host primarily in AWS but extend to multi-cloud as well. From cloud-native, cloud-only to hybrid and multi-cloud, OSRS Group is your long-term partner for building, hosting and sustainment.

Datasets or data infrastructure

We build analytic data sets and provide as Data as a Service (DaaS) for our clients in conjunction with our service offerings. The bulk of our data services are geospatial, temporal or spatio-temporal in nature at (US) national and global scale. Many of our datasets are derived analytic outputs arising in part from public data using a combination of well-known techniques, AI/ML and OSRS Group custom approaches.

Subject matter expertise

We have subject matter expertise in computer science, AI / data science, geoinformatics, utility infrastructure, supply chains, environment and defense domains. Our staff has worked across top organizations including: AECOM, Booz Allen Hamilton, DoD, General Atomics, intelligence agencies, NASA, Pacific Northwest National Lab, Raytheon, University of Virginia, and many others.
