Introduction

Genesis Mission Alignment: Modern cyber-physical systems generate massive, complex data volumes from electrical, mechanical, optical, chemical sensors, which need to be ingested, normalized, contextualized, visualized, modeled, analyzed and compared to simulation. Speeding up science and technology requires across America's national priorities requires a professional-grade engineering platform with built-in traceability and explainability, which is precisely what Falkonry does.

Falkonry's commercial-off-the-shelf (COTS) Time-Series AI platform designed to process massive volumes of high-frequency telemetry data powered by its proprietary self-supervised, deep learning technology, called PatternIQ™. It works with heterogeneous, unsynchronized data streams with varying sampling rates and doesn't require data engineering. Its processing scales from enterprise clouds down to the operational edge.

Falkonry’s platform has a proven track record in high-consequence defense and industrial environments. The U.S. Navy validated it for power system analysis and anomaly detection on advanced naval platforms. Commercially, it has delivered millions in productivity improvements and prevented hundreds of hours of downtime in energy-intensive industries like steel manufacturing and semiconductor fabrication. Falkonry is an In-Q-Tel portfolio company and the winner of the AIST Hunt-Kelly Award and the AFWERX STRATFI program, and was featured in the CBInsights AI 100 and Forbes AI 50 lists.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

The Falkonry AI platform is a high-speed, scalable time-series data processing platform that ingests, normalizes, aligns, enriches, analyzes, and visualizes massive volumes of heterogeneous sensor data. It unifies multiple sensor sources and third-party analytic modules into a single processing architecture.

Its core capabilities include:
1. High-speed ingestion and time alignment
2. Signal enrichment and deep feature extraction
3. Deep anomaly detection and pattern recognition
4. Error handling and signal denoising
5. Alerting, event detection, and visualization

AI/ML/Data science expertise

Falkonry offers specialized machine learning and AI expertise in the processing of time series data arising from high speed sensors in physical systems and their simulations. Falkonry has developed fast learning systems from variational convolutional encoders that provide embeddings for custom task models in a multi-head architecture enabling rapid development of anomaly detection, classification, forecasting, and optimization models. The underlying embedding generation has been battle tested against a variety of industrial data sets in the real-world during commercial, paid applications.

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

The platform is designed for flexible deployment and integration. It can operate as a headless analytics service independent of specific workflow systems, integrating via REST interfaces, message queues, agents, and structured data formats like JSON or Parquet. It features a horizontally scalable architecture that supports both resource-constrained edge deployments and larger server and high performance computing environments.

Project Idea Summary

Falkonry is a domain-agnostic software expert with specialized knowhow of time series data and AI methods of exploiting large volumes of such data, which occurs all over the physical world. 

Falkonry believes that real-time data processing is key to rapid success of teams pursuing topics 1-D, 16-B, 21-C, 5-G, 1-F, 13-A, 13-B, 6-C, 11-C, 17-C, 16-A and 6-B. Therefore, Falkonry can be a part of any such team and provide it the most complete analytical platform for their real-time data whether it originates in decades of testing, from simulation or from real-world operations. 

The main benefit of Falkonry is that it speeds up the time to first result and doubles the success rate of analytics projects by systematically eliminating disjointed data capture, hidden failure in data collection, the metadata void and the deployment lag.

Falkonry is therefore seeking to collaborate with domain and research experts leading the above topics so it can help transition research to industrial operations.
