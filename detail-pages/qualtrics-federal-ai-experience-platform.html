    Introduction

    Qualtrics is an experience and AI insights platform supporting 250+ federal agencies, including DOE, to measure and optimize workforce performance, program effectiveness, and technology adoption. In the context of the Genesis Mission, we serve as the “measurement and validation layer” for AI helping teams quantify impact, accelerate adoption, and demonstrate outcomes across scientific workflows, operations, and workforce transformation.

    Offerings & Experience

    Offerings
    AI models, tools, or software

    Qualtrics provides an AI-powered experience and analytics platform that combines survey-based data collection, unstructured text analytics, and predictive modeling into a unified system. Our platform enables real-time insights, automated workflows, and decision support across workforce, operational, and scientific environments. We support use cases such as digital twin feedback loops, AI-assisted decisioning, and continuous performance optimization.

    AI/ML/Data science expertise

    We bring advanced AI/ML capabilities including natural language processing (NLP), sentiment and intent classification, predictive analytics, and synthetic respondent modeling. These capabilities enable large-scale analysis of qualitative and quantitative data, helping organizations identify patterns, forecast outcomes, and simulate user or workforce responses to AI-driven changes.

    Datasets or data infrastructure

    Qualtrics enables the collection, integration, and analysis of structured and unstructured data across workforce, user, and operational domains. Our platform creates continuous feedback loops that capture real-time signals from employees, stakeholders, and systems, enabling unified data environments that support AI model validation, performance tracking, and iterative improvement.

    Subject matter expertise

We understand federal data, security, and compliance requirements and align solutions to mission-driven outcomes and reporting needs.

    Relevant Projects

    1. U.S. Department of Energy – Headquarters & Enterprise Workforce Analytics

    Scope: Deployed Qualtrics across DOE Headquarters to support workforce insights, human capital analytics, and program performance measurement using a combination of structured and unstructured data. 
    Achievements: Enabled enterprise-wide visibility into workforce engagement and operational performance; reduced manual reporting through real-time dashboards; supported leadership decision-making with AI-driven text analytics and predictive insights across large-scale federal programs.

    2. Sandia National Laboratories – Workforce Experience & AI-Ready Feedback Systems

    Scope: Implemented an enterprise employee experience (EX) platform supporting over 16,000 users, integrating lifecycle feedback, analytics, and guided success programs to improve workforce performance and engagement. 
    Achievements: Scaled a centralized feedback and analytics system across a national lab environment; enabled continuous workforce insights and lifecycle tracking; established a foundation for integrating AI-driven analytics and workforce adoption measurement in mission-critical environments.

    3. Oak Ridge National Laboratory – Integrated CX/EX & Operational Insights

    Scope: Deployed Qualtrics across multiple domains including employee experience, customer experience, and digital feedback, integrating with enterprise systems (e.g., SAP) to support onboarding/off boarding and operational workflows. 
    Achievements: Enabled cross-functional insights across workforce and user experience; improved operational efficiency through integrated data collection and analytics; supported real-time decision-making and continuous improvement across lab operations.

    Project Idea Summary

    We are pursuing a Genesis Mission application focused on “Achieving AI-Driven Autonomous Laboratories” and “AI for Scientific Reasoning,” with the goal of enabling measurable AI advantage across scientific workflows. Our approach integrates AI models, digital workflows, and a measurement and validation layer that captures workforce adoption, human-in-the-loop interactions, and program performance outcomes in real time.

    Qualtrics will provide the experience and analytics platform to collect and analyze signals from users, systems, and processes, enabling teams to identify bottlenecks, optimize AI-enabled workflows, and demonstrate quantifiable improvements in efficiency, accuracy, and speed of discovery. This supports scalable deployment of AI-driven capabilities across lab and operational environments.

    We are seeking partners with strengths in AI/ML model development, scientific computing, and domain expertise (e.g., energy, nuclear, or laboratory operations), as well as access to relevant datasets and experimental environments. Ideal partners include DOE national laboratories, academic institutions, and industry organizations developing AI-driven scientific workflows, digital twins, or autonomous systems.
