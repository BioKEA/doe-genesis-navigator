Introduction

I am an experimental plasma physicist and interested in basic and applied plasma physics research. My current projects include FLARE (Facility for Laboratory Reconnection Experiments) at Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory and the project website is https://www.pppl.gov/FLARE. I am interested in AI/ML applications to large datasets that FLARE generates on magnetic reconnection and related phenomena, which occurs in fusion, space and astrophysical plasmas. I am eager to get connected with industrial partners with whom I can collaborate on AI/ML applications.

Capabilities Seeking
AI models, tools, or software

Multi-model capabilities that combining information from different types of datasets (two movies from different angles, and many in-situ measurements) to identify dynamic 3D objects.

Project Idea Summary

We have 3 challenges: (1) combing information from global morphological measurements from 2 different angles and local in-situ measurements to identify dynamic 3D objects; (2) using physics-informed AI/ML tools to extract missing but critical data from other existing data; (3) efficiently identify 3D objects from a massive but still limited local in-situ measurements distributed in 3D space. The model can be trained by simulation data which is limited due to its high costs.
