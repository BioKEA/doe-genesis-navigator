    Introduction

    My interests are in the development of atomistic, quantum, and machine learning methods for computing properties of materials, and application of these methods to understand and design materials systems for energy storage, microelectronics, and catalysis applications.

    I designed and co-led the development the AiiDA workflow automation infrastructure.  https://aiida.net/
    Our group group conceived and implemented the architecture underpinning leading machine learning interatomic potentials and foundation models - NequIP, Allegro (https://nequip.net), GnoMe, SevenNet, MACE, ESEN, UMA, etc. We also introduced new ML models (CIDER) for describing electronic exchange and correlation, in order to enhance the speed and fidelity of density functional theory (DFT) calculations for materials (seminconductors, catalysts, magnets, battery electrodes, ferroelectrics) where electronic structure needs careful treatment beyond conventional semilocal approximations.

    Offerings & Experience

    Offerings
    AI models, tools, or software

    Our group group pioneered the architecture underpinning leading machine learning interatomic potentials and foundation models - NequIP, Allegro (https://nequip.net), GnoMe, SevenNet, MACE, ESEN, UMA, etc. We also created new ML models (CIDER) for describing electronic exchange and correlation, in order to enhance the speed and fidelity of density functional theory (DFT) calculations for materials (seminconductors, catalysts, magnets, battery electrodes, ferroelectrics) where electronic structure needs to be treated carefully, beyond typical approximations. 

    AI/ML/Data science expertise

    Development and application of ML/AI methods for accelerated computational design of materials. Autonomous generation of training data sets with active learing (FLARE, https://github.com/mir-group/flare). I am interested in autonomous and agentic computational workflows for materials simulations and design. I designed and implemented the AiiDA data provenance and workflow automation framework (https://aiida.net) and am developing data pipelines for ML training and deployment.
