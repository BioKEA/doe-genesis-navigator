Introduction

Accenture is a global leader in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Mission Reinvention, employing over 779,000 strategists, technologists, and domain experts across 120+ countries to serve 9,000+ clients in nearly all major industries and core business functions. Accenture Federal Services, our U.S.-based subsidiary, brings these capabilities to missions of national importance. With more than 13,000 employees and 30+ years of experience supporting the U.S. Government, we deliver technology-driven outcomes while meeting stringent security and compliance requirements.

A trusted partner of the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) and National Laboratories for over 10 years, Accenture is proud to be among the initial Genesis Mission industry partners and is committed to serving as a solution integrator. In this role, we integrate leading technologies, data, and stakeholders to deliver scalable, cohesive outcomes. Our focus is on providing solutions that scale across DOE and the Genesis ecosystem through the following services: program management; platform strategy, onboarding, and optimization; data curation and management; scientific workflow automation; and mission adoption. Accenture is excited to offer an integrated discovery platform (currently in Beta) to Genesis partners that unifies leading technology solutions to accelerate scientific research.

We have active contracts with nearly every federal scientific agency, including CDC, NOAA, USSF. At DOE, this includes our CBOSS BPA.

Relevant Projects

#1: Data Curation & Management: Accenture operates one of the largest secure multi-domain data ecosystems in the federal Defense sector. This environment supports more than 7,000 mission users who rely on consistent, trusted access to massive‑scale data ingestion, fusion, and high‑velocity analytics to drive daily operational decision‑making. To meet these demands, we implemented comprehensive automation for data hygiene, classification, and enrichment across multiple security domains, ensuring that information moves rapidly and securely from collection to mission application. 

To further enhance performance, Accenture deployed a cutting‑edge GPU infrastructure designed to dramatically accelerate artificial intelligence and machine‑learning workloads—achieving performance gains of up to 6,000 %. These improvements enable advanced analytics, modeling, and real‑time insights that were previously unattainable at this scale.

#2: Automating Scientific Workflows: A specialty chemicals company partnered with Accenture to reinvent its end‑to‑end Idea‑to‑Launch research process, unlocking significant value and driving more than $330 million in top‑line growth. To begin the transformation, Accenture conducted a comprehensive assessment of existing workflows, capability gaps, and operational challenges across the research lifecycle. This work identified several “must‑win” opportunities that, if addressed, would materially improve innovation speed, research productivity, and commercial impact.

Building on these insights, Accenture embedded artificial intelligence into critical research stages, introducing new tools, decision‑support capabilities, and data‑driven processes that enhanced how teams generate, assess, and advance ideas. Accenture also established clear performance benchmarks to measure improvement, prioritized the highest‑value AI use cases, and developed an actionable enablement roadmap that aligned technology investments with strategic business goals.

Through this integrated approach, the company achieved measurable and sustained transformation. Research activities became more efficient, insights became more predictive, and teams were able to focus on higher‑value scientific work. As a result, the organization doubled the sales impact of its research pipeline and built a scalable foundation for continuous innovation and long‑term growth

#3: Platform Onboarding & Optimization: Accenture partnered with a federal defense agency to deliver a secure, fully managed cloud platform that of restricted-access programs to rapidly deploy and scale 400+ mission workloads. Through a customized implementation, the platform achieved authorization across multiple classification regions, including the highest national security levels, providing a consistent, trusted foundation for sensitive mission operations. This environment allows restricted‑access programs to onboard to the cloud 50%+ faster while maintaining the rigorous security controls required for highly classified work.

Accenture transformed the traditional authorization process by shifting to a dynamic, inheritance‑based model. Customers inherit real‑time implementation directly from the authorized platform, reducing duplication and accelerating approval timelines. Our team developed comprehensive cybersecurity compliance packages, including the first traceability matrix of its kind, and collaborated closely to define corrective actions and establish continuous monitoring.

Project Idea Summary

Accenture is building a network of partners across industry, national laboratories, academia, nonprofits, and independent research groups to accelerate scientific discovery. These partners contribute mission critical capabilities, ranging from compute infrastructure and cloud environments to datasets that power advanced research. 

We are building a reusable foundation to offload non-research tasks, enabling scientists to focus on discovery and accelerate progress through turn-key offerings and standardized frameworks. This includes an integrated discovery platform that unifies diverse resources in a secure, scalable environment, enabling researchers to access and use them with ease. 

We seek collaborators aligned to the following S&T challenge areas (Grid Reliability & Energy Security, Science Enablement & Autonomous Labs, Supply Chain Optimization, Next Generation Nuclear & Small Modular Reactors) to jointly drive scientific outcomes. We also welcome partners from other S&T domains interested in leveraging a unified platform to accelerate discovery, access DOE and other Mission collaborators datasets, and utilize collaborative development capabilities offered by our platform and data services. By combining our integration expertise with our partners’ assets, we create an ecosystem where scientists can stay focused on discovery, while the underlying technology operates reliably, securely, and at scale.
