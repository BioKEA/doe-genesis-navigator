    Introduction

    Amit Chourasia is a Chief Research Data Architect at UCLA, where he leads OneSciencePlace - a new compute portal/science gateway framework and the Quakeworx science gateway. Earlier, he was the Director of Software and Platforms at the San Diego Supercomputer Center at UC San Diego. He led the platforms, including Hubzero®, SeedMeLab, and the NAIRR Pilot Portal.

    Interested in partnering to develop and deliver scalable compute and data platforms for various science-driven use cases.
