    Introduction

    I am an assistant professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Rice University. My areas of expertise are in control theory, machine learning, and optimization with applications to the reliable control of engineering systems including autonomous vehicles and robotic applications. I am seeking collaborators for robotics research.

    Project Idea Summary

    I am looking to partner with someone who is willing to explore a collaboration in bringing autonomous robots to DoE-style labs. I have a collaborators at Rice University with expertise in (i) generating high-level plans from human-interpretable specifications, (ii) generating motion plans from specifications, and (iii) my expertise in reliably executing these plans in a closed-loop fashion. While we have some tabletop robots we can run rudimentary experiments on, we are looking to collaborate with domain-experts who could take advantage of autonomous robots in their lab.
