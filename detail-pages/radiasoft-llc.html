Introduction

RadiaSoft delivers state-of-the art browser-based control systems for particle accelerators. Our services integrate high performance computation with real-time operations for peak facility performance. RadiaSoft is a leader in accelerator system design and optimization. We support commissioning and new facility design for national laboratories, universities and industry.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

Model development and deployment using Tensorflow and PyTorch. Expertise with mlops using bespoke solutions and mlflow. 

AI/ML/Data science expertise

Development of novel models for particle accelerator and medical technology. General focus on diagnostics for both real-time and near-real-time applications. RF signal processing models, surrogate models for computing, and anomaly detection models. Expertise with both large and small datasets including sparse datasets. 

Computing infrastructure or cloud resources

RadiaSoft services an in house computing cluster hosted using Jupyter hub. We deploy servers using Docker images both on our local computing and using high performance computing at NERSC. We have expertise in distributed computing using bespoke solutions and kubernetes.  

Datasets or data infrastructure

Expertise in large scale and small datasets for large and medium scale accelerator systems for scientific discovery, high energy physics, basic energy science, nuclear physics, and biological science. 

Experimental or test facilities

We have expertise working with all scales of test facilities. We have active projects with every large scale accelerator facility in the US and some overseas. We support small scale facilities both laboratory and industrial systems including control system integration and data science. We have expertise with both sides of operational accelerators (the user end stations and the accelerator operations). 

Subject matter expertise

Accelerator physics, plasma physics, neutron science, magnet technology, RF technology, and fusion technology. Our scientists have a wide range of skills in high performance computing and system design for large and small experimental systems.
