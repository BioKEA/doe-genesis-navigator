Introduction

My research group pursues 3D photonic-electronic computing systems for AI applications.

We are capable of the following:

(a) We conduct research on new generation of nanoscale photonic and electronic devices co-integrated to enable extremely energy efficient and high-throughput integrated circuits.  Examples include photonic crystal lasers integrated with nanophotonic detectors with nano FETs to form an optoelectronic neurons aiming at ~10 attojoules/bit.  

(b) We co-design and co-integrate 3D photonic-electronic integrated circuits exploiting best of both worlds' (photonics and electronics) AI circuits with high throughput, high scalability, and diverse functionality.  In particular, we have been designing and fabricating 3D photonic circuits co-integrated with 3D electronics.

(c) We have been investigating a new generation of computing systems exploiting 3D photonic-electronic integrated circuits scaling out and scaling up to brain-like AI computing systems.  Systems integration and performance benchmarking under various AI workloads are also being pursued.

(d) In parallel to the above photonic-electronics for AI, we have also been utilizing AI for designing photonic-electronic devices.

We are interested in collaborating and participating as part of a team in the related area.  Or we may like to form a team in partnership with researchers of complementary skills.

Please feel free to contact me at sbyoo@ucdavis.edu.
