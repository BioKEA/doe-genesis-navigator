    Introduction

    Jorge Fonseca, Ph.D., is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science, Howard R. Hughes College of Engineering, University of Nevada, Las Vegas focused on applying artificial intelligence to multiple domains including neuroscience, public health, healthcare, nursing, drones, and early childhood development.

    Examples of current collaborations include Alzheimer’s disease clinical trial research with Jeffrey L. Cummings, neuroscience with Jingchun Chen, COVID-19 wastewater surveillance with Edwin Oh through the Nevada EMPOWER program, midwifery regulation and maternity care outcomes with Jennifer Vanderlaan, and early childhood language development with Sarah Surrain. All with an emphasis on building practical, deployable AI systems.
