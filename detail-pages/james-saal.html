Introduction

Citrine Informatics’ role in the DOE Genesis Mission can be understood through its core expertise in AI-driven materials discovery and data infrastructure, which aligns closely with the mission’s goals. Citrine Informatics, as a leader in AI for materials and chemicals development, provides platforms that organize complex materials data and apply machine learning to dramatically reduce R&D time and uncover new material candidates. In this context, we represent the type of private-sector partner envisioned by the Genesis Mission: contributing AI models, data-centric workflows, and domain-specific expertise that help transform traditional trial-and-error materials research into faster, AI-guided discovery systems. By enabling scalable, data-driven materials innovation, our capabilities directly support the Genesis Mission’s objective of compressing years of scientific progress into months and advancing next-generation energy and materials solutions.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

The Citrine Platform provides an enterprise-grade, AI-native environment for structuring materials data, building predictive models, and running large-scale virtual experiments to identify optimal material candidates and formulations. The Platform enables partners to seamlessly connect data, simulation, and autonomous experimentation, accelerating materials discovery while reducing cost, risk, and development timelines.

AI/ML/Data science expertise

Citrine Informatics brings deep expertise in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science applied to materials and chemical R&D, with a focus on transforming fragmented experimental and simulation data into actionable insights. Its approach combines structured data infrastructure, physics-aware machine learning models, and generative AI to enable prediction of material properties, optimization of formulations, and design of next-generation materials with fewer experiments.

Subject matter expertise

Citrine Informatics brings extensive subject matter expertise spanning a wide range of materials classes and chemical systems, including polymers, metals, ceramics, coatings, batteries, specialty chemicals, and advanced composites. This expertise is grounded in deep experience across both R&D and manufacturing environments. By combining domain knowledge with data science and AI, Citrine supports industry partners in sectors such as energy, aerospace, automotive, electronics, and consumer products, helping translate complex materials behavior into deployable, high-performance solutions.

Project Idea Summary

Citrine Informatics is actively seeking partners with Genesis-aligned challenges where advanced AI/ML and data-driven approaches can meaningfully accelerate discovery and development. We are particularly interested in collaborations involving complex materials and chemical systems, where fragmented data, long development cycles, or scale-up uncertainties limit progress. By applying the Citrine Platform’s capabilities in data structuring, predictive modeling, and virtual experimentation, we aim to work with partners to de-risk innovation, uncover high-performing candidates more efficiently, and contribute to the DOE Genesis Mission’s vision of integrated, AI-enabled scientific advancement.
