Introduction

We believe the primary determinant of success for Genesis will be the ability to transform and supplement often heterogeneous, sensitive, and compartmented scientific and engineering data, into trusted, AI-ready data assets that can be safely used and continuously improved within classified environments for model training, tuning, simulation, and evaluation.

Genesis will require a repeatable, secure capability to prepare data, create and maintain evaluation artifacts, and govern AI system development over time as models and missions evolve. As the leader in data-centric AI, Snorkel is best positioned to support on this journey, leveraging 10+ years of experience supporting the majority of frontier AI laboratories and numerous Fortune 500 enterprises in large-scale data curation, model training, and evaluation across the full machine learning lifecycle with deep roots in AI data research and programmatic data development.

Snorkel is a global leader in data-centric AI, founded in the Stanford AI Lab and backed by more than 100 peer-reviewed publications and best-paper awards at NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, UAI, and VLDB. Our methods for dataset curation, weak supervision, quality control, verification labeling, expert-in-the-loop evaluation, and more have shaped how frontier labs and F500 enterprises build and measure large AI systems. We complement this academic research foundation with years of experience delivering AI data and outcomes at scale to leading frontier AI Labs & Government

Offerings & Experience

AI models, tools, or software

Snorkel AI leverages its expertise in delivering hardened enterprise AI solutions that are built to customer’s specs, leveraging the large language model and hardware investments already made. Some examples of custom made solutions can be found here: https://snorkel.ai/customer-stories/

This further highlights Snorkel AI’s expertise, from providing a platform capable of having DOE and its Labs leverage to create training data for high accuracy specialized synthetic training data generation, to further the frontier of said models as it relates to the Genesis Mission. 

AI/ML/Data science expertise

Snorkel operates as a true research and engineering partner. Snorkel leads one of the strongest expert contributor networks in the industry, built from the ground up around experts. This foundation gives us a fundamental edge in recruiting, retaining, and training experts like experienced scientists, software engineers, ML practitioners, and domain specialists who partner with customer SME’s to author complex custom  mission-specific datasets. This expertise is paired with our capabilities, which provides controls and expertise for data curation and structuring to outcomes.
