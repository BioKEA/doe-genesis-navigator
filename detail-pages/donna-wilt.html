    Introduction

    Noblis, Inc. is an AI-forward non-profit science and technology innovation company. For more than 30 years we have applied our expertise in science, technology, and engineering to develop independent and objective solutions.   Together with our subsidiaries, Noblis ESI (Engineering Systems for Intelligence) and Noblis MSD (Mission Solutions for Defense), we work closely with customers to support and accelerate defense, federal civil, homeland security, intelligence and law enforcement missions. Noblis ESI focuses on high-quality systems engineering, advanced technology, program management and acquisition services enabling Command, Control, Computers, Communications, Cyber, Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (C5ISR) mission success. Noblis MSD provides leading-edge network design, systems integration, sustainment and modernization solutions and operational readiness for the U.S. Navy. As a system developer, Noblis MSD brings advanced engineering, enterprise transformation and program management solutions to improve operations, maintenance and modernization of equipment and systems throughout their life cycle. 

    Our current Genesis Mission partner interests are: operationally relevant data to bench mark our R&D proven AI-forward ideas in accelerating nuclear reactor time to market; predicting and scaling of new complex materials design; and weather prediction acuracy; and use cases for our foundational leading-edge AI model developments and bioengineering methods.
