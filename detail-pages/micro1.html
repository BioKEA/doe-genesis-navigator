Introduction

micro1: The Data Infrastructure for AGI

micro1 is the high-performance data engine that empowers AI, bridging the gap between raw model potential and real-world execution. We build, deploy, and evaluate autonomous agents that are safe, accountable, and mission-ready. Serving as the essential infrastructure for the transition to AGI, micro1 is the trusted partner of frontier AI labs and Magnificent Seven, providing the expert-driven data required to refine and scale advanced models.

Our impact spans both the public and private sectors. We embed elite LLM engineers within high-stakes environments including CDAO to validate rigorous delivery models, then scale these high-security frameworks to the global commercial market. By managing everything from use-case scoping to technical validation, we ensure AI systems remain strictly aligned with human intent and mission requirements.

This mission is fueled by a network of 130,000+ vetted experts across 100+ domains. Recently adding 2,000+ U.S.-based specialists, including legal, financial, and technical experts, we provide high-fidelity feedback for next-generation AI training. From federal workflows to enterprise automation, micro1 is turning AI into a scalable utility.

Offerings & Experience

Offerings
AI models, tools, or software

Realm (RL Environments): A proprietary training environment where AI agents operate within simulated, real-world scenarios. We provide targeted reward signals and verifiable feedback loops, driven by our network of 130,000+ vetted domain experts, to improve model reasoning, planning, and tool-use.

Cortex (Evaluation Framework): A suite of contextual evaluation tools grounded in expert-level human data. Cortex allows for stress-testing and benchmarking AI agents against complex, domain-specific workflows (e.g., legal analysis, financial auditing, and tax compliance).

AI/ML/Data science expertise

micro1’s AI/ML expertise is anchored by a technical team from top schools and research institutions. We specialize in advanced Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) and the development of proprietary training environments like Realm.  Our technical focus includes developing high-signal reward models, scalable evaluation frameworks (Cortex), and automated red-teaming to ensure models are robust, safe, and strictly aligned with rigorous government standards.

Datasets or data infrastructure

micro1 provides a vertically integrated end-to-end data engine that transforms human expertise into high-signal AI training datasets. Our platform manages the entire lifecycle: Zara (AI-powered sourcing/vetting) identifies the top 1% of domain experts; our Human Data Platform handles multimodal production (SFT, RLHF, Red-teaming); and Merit provides real-time quality telemetry. We deliver structured, model-ready data via a seamless Data API, currently powering frontier labs and the Mag 7 with the "Chain of Thought" reasoning required for mission-ready AGI.

Subject matter expertise

Our subject matter expertise lies in the last mile of AGI: building autonomous agents powered by a integrated data engine. We manage a global network of 130,000+ vetted experts,. including a rapidly growing base of  legal, financial, and technical specialists to provide the high-fidelity feedback required for complex reasoning and tool-use. micro1's platform handles the end-to-end collection, labeling, and delivery of non-scrapeable human judgment. This infrastructure ensures that AI agents can be deployed as reliable, mission-ready utilities across both government and commercial sectors.

Relevant Projects

Past Project Experience: CDAO Agentic AI Pilot & Operational Scaling

Scope of Work: micro1 is executing a high-impact pilot program for the Chief Digital and Artificial Intelligence Office (CDAO) focused on the end-to-end automation of core mission workflows. We have deployed embedded AI engineers directly within the CDAO to build, validate, and scale agentic AI systems. This work involves mapping complex government processes and replacing manual bottlenecks with autonomous agents capable of handling multi-step reasoning, policy-compliant decision-making, and real-time data synthesis across all operational levels.

Achievements: Successfully established a repeatable AI Delivery Model that transitions models from sandboxed prototypes to production-ready utilities. By automating critical administrative and analytical processes, the pilot has demonstrated a measurable reduction in lead times for mission-critical tasks. Our "embedded expertise" approach has allowed the CDAO to deploy core agentic AI that remains strictly aligned with Department of Defense (DoD) safety standards, providing a scalable blueprint for government-wide AI adoption.

 

Zara – AI-Powered Expert Sourcing & Vetting

Scope of Work: micro1 developed and deployed Zara, an autonomous AI recruiter, to build a global infrastructure of subject-matter expertise. The project involved creating dynamic, conversational LLM-based interviewers capable of evaluating technical "expert affinity" across 100+ domains (Legal, Finance, PhD-level Research, etc.).

Achievements: Successfully scaled a network of 130,000+ vetted experts. Zara reduced the average time-to-hire for ultra-niche specialists from weeks to under 48 hours, maintaining a top-1% selectivity rate. This infrastructure now serves as the primary data-sourcing engine for frontier AI labs.

 

Realm – Reinforcement Learning (RL) Training Environments

Scope of Work: micro1 designed and implemented Realm, a series of high-fidelity RL "gyms" and training environments. The scope included building simulated, real-world scenarios where AI agents could operate under expert-defined constraints to generate high-signal reward data and "Chain of Thought" reasoning.

Achievements: Provided a core training environment used by Frontier AI Labs and the Magnificent Seven to bridge the "sim-to-real" gap. The project successfully transitioned models from static chat interfaces to agentic systems capable of executing complex, multi-step professional workflows with verifiable accuracy.

Project Idea Summary

micro1: Autonomous Agentic Infrastructure for DOE

micro1 proposes a specialized Data and Agentic Infrastructure to solve the Department of Energy’s dual challenges in legacy data recovery and frontier scientific discovery. We deploy autonomous agents to transform unstructured historical records into actionable intelligence and accelerate scientific reasoning via expert-reinforced training loops.

Area 1: Harnessing Historic Nuclear Data

The Challenge: Eight decades of nuclear research are locked in legacy documents that are costly and slow to review for Export Controlled Information (ECI) or safety-basis requirements.

The Solution: We deploy specialized agents to autonomously digitize and review these records. Allowing agents to act as autonomous compliance officers that suggest appropriate markings and ensure strict DOE protocol adherence at scale.

Area 2: AI for Scientific Reasoning

The Challenge: LLMs struggle to synthesize disparate scientific sources and simulation data into novel, testable theories.

The Solution: We implement agentic workflows within our Realm RL environment to query DOE libraries and integrate real-time simulation feedback. By building a dataset of symbolic proofs and logical chains labeled by our 130,000+ vetted experts, we fine-tune models for rigorous deduction and hypothesis generation to identify knowledge "dark spots."
