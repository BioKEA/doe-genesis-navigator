    Introduction

    The Efficient Computing Systems Laboratory (ECS Lab) is a research group focused on building energy- and resource-efficient computing systems, spanning algorithms, software, and architecture. Our goal is to co-design hardware and software to enable practical AI and data-driven applications under tight constraints (compute, memory, energy, and latency), from the edge to large-scale computing platforms.
